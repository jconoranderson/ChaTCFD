The SynergE6 Healthspan Model
SynergE6 establishes a unified, integrated framework where medicine, science, therapeutic environments and an active lifestyle work together to promote better health outcomes, build stronger communities, and empower individuals to lead fuller, more meaningful lives.

The SynergE6 Model includes six core, interconnected components:

Evaluation
Comprehensive, interdisciplinary assessment of medical, developmental, emotional, and environmental needs to create highly individualized care plans.

Environment
Thoughtfully designed physical, temporal, and social spaces that reduce sensory stress, support regulation, and promote engagement, healing, and independence.

Eating and Nutrition
Nutritionally balanced, therapeutic diets tailored to medical needs, designed to optimize gut health, support immune function, regulate metabolism, and enhance overall well-being.

Energy Regulation
Structured physical activity, movement therapies, and daily routines that help individuals regulate arousal levels, improve stamina, and maintain a balanced mind-body connection and promote good sleep.

Emotional Regulation
Targeted therapies and supports that build emotional resilience, reduce anxiety, and teach adaptive coping strategies critical for social success and mental health.

Evidence-Based Education and Treatment
Proven, research-driven educational strategies and therapeutic interventions focused on functional skill-building, communication, autonomy, and lifelong learning.

Together, these six elements operate synergistically to create a powerful, dynamic system of care - one that fosters healing from the inside out, addressing not only medical symptoms but also enhancing daily living, emotional well-being, and social participation.
The SynergE6 Healthspan Model reflects the future of health and human services: a seamless, holistic approach where individuals thrive because their care is comprehensive, connected, and deeply rooted in evidence and compassion.
